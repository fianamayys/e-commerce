FIMs Nocturne – Eau de Parfum

A Fresh & Soothing Scent – Like the First Breath of Winter.


FIMs Nocturne delivers a clean and invigorating sensation — perfect for those who crave freedom and simplicity. This fragrance evokes the feeling of walking through pure white snow, with crisp, cooling air that refreshes the soul.


[✿.Characteristics] Mint, Icy Lemon, Eucalyptus, White Tea, Lavender, Snow Lily, Cedarwood, Musk, Soft Amber.

[✿.Vibes] Cool, clean, and composed. Ideal for morning routines, business meetings, or anytime you want to appear sharp and sophisticated.

FIMs Celestial – Eau de Parfum

A Sweet, Classic & Elegant Scent – The Timeless Essence of Spring.


Celestial is a fragrance that feels luxurious yet soft — reminiscent of a blooming garden in early spring. Its sweet notes feel like warm, nostalgic memories. Perfect for those who exude grace and love a touch of vintage charm.


[✿.Characteristics] Bergamot, Peach Blossom, Pink Pepper, Rose, Iris, Orange Flower, Vanilla, Tonka Bean, White Musk.

[✿.Vibes] Romantic, classic, and elegant. Ideal for formal occasions, dinner dates, or when you want to feel like the main character in a timeless love story.

FIMs Voluèntic – Eau de Parfum

A Gentle & Deep Scent – Like a Quiet Afternoon in Full Bloom.


Voluèntic is a delicate blend of softness and depth. Its fragrance recalls roses picked at dusk, layered with the warmth of brewed coffee and damp woods after the rain. Subtle but unforgettable.

[✿.Characteristics] Turkish Rose, Pink Pepper, Coffee, Black Tea, Orris, Sandalwood, Cashmere Wood, Light Incense.

[✿.Vibes] Soft, calm, and intimate. Best worn in the afternoon, during creative focus, or in reflective, quiet moments.
